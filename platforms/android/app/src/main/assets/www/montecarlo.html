Info on track Another great bike tour to discover two beautiful, charming medieval Tuscan villages. In Montecarlo possibility to make a Wine Tasting in a local farm , to taste the great Tuscan red wines. Arrived in Montecatini Alto, after exploring the historic center and the church,  you can decide to return to the spa town thanks to the funicular.
