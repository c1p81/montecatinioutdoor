Nani Svizzeri

 

These tracks have been created by an association called “NANI SVIZZERI”, since they are located in the Valleriana of Pescia, also known as the “Switzerland” of Pescia; they are partly exhisting CAI tracks, or old medieval paths called “mulattiere”, that means the old road for farmers and their animals, or parts of abandoned woods. Most of them are located between Castelvecchio, San Quirico, Mount Battifolle and “Croce a Veglia” over Pontito. All the tracks are single tracks of average length, with no particularly hard obstacles: for starters we recommend much attention and all the safety protections.
