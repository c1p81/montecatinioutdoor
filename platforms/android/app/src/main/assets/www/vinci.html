The ring tour of Vinci

 

Difficulty level
Lenght 46 Km
Gap 1973 + / 1973 -
Season All year
Ground Gravel road, trail, paved road
Description stile roadbook
Info on track It consists of a wonderful but demanding  ring tour  of about 45 km for about 2000 mt of difference in altitude. There are no technical stretches but you definitely be in shape if you want to try. Leaving Vinci you head towards the Montalbano and then reach Pietra Marina, ancient Etruscan place. You then head towards the hills in Limite sull’Arno. Finally you go upwards back to Vinci.
